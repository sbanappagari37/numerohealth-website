FAQ
Is NumeroHealth medical advice? ▾

No. NumeroHealth is an educational wellness tool. It doesn't diagnose, treat, or prescribe, and it isn't a substitute for your healthcare provider. Always talk to your provider about your medication and health decisions.

Do I need a prescription to use it? ▾

No prescription is required to use NumeroHealth. It's designed for people who are already using — or preparing to use — GLP-1 medications under the care of their own provider.

Which medications does it support? ▾

NumeroHealth is designed for people on GLP-1 medications. You tell the app which one you're on during setup, and it tailors your experience from there.

Is my information private? ▾

Yes. We take your privacy seriously and we don't sell your personal data. You can read the details in our Privacy Policy.
